# Starter Prompt: Step 1 — Product Overview

Use this when your IDEATION.md is filled in and you're ready to synthesize it into a PRODUCT_OVERVIEW.md.

---

## Prompt

```
I've finished my initial ideation for a project. Here's my IDEATION.md:

[paste the contents of your IDEATION.md here]

Now I want to create a PRODUCT_OVERVIEW.md that answers the five W questions about my product:
- Who is this for?
- What does it do?
- Where does it live (mobile, web, desktop)?
- When — what's the timeline?
- Why does it need to exist?

Please read my ideation and then ask me focused questions to fill in each of these sections.
Go one section at a time. Start with Who.
```

---

## Tips

- If Claude's summary of a section doesn't sound like you, correct it: "That's not quite right — what I meant was..."
- Claude should be drawing out *your* answers, not inventing them. If it's putting words in your mouth, push back.
- The Non-Goals section is important — ask Claude: "Based on everything we discussed, what should I explicitly list as out of scope?"

---

## When You're Done

Read the completed PRODUCT_OVERVIEW.md out loud to yourself. If every sentence still sounds like your product, you're ready for Step 2. If anything feels off, fix it before moving on — everything downstream depends on this document being accurate.

Then start a new session with the [Step 2 Design prompt](./level-2-design.md).
