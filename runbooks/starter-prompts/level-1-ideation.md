# Starter Prompt: Step 1 — Ideation

Use this when you're starting fresh and want to get your ideas out of your head and into `IDEATION.md`.

---

## Prompt

```
I'm starting a new project and I want to fill out my IDEATION.md file.

Here's what I'm thinking so far:
[paste your rough ideas here — a few sentences is enough to start]

Please help me explore and expand on these ideas. I want you to:
1. Ask me questions to draw out more detail — especially about who this is for and what problem it solves
2. Help me think through things I might not have considered yet
3. NOT jump to solutions or technical details yet — this is just a brain dump

Start by asking me your first question.
```

---

## When You Have Nothing Yet

If you're truly starting from zero, use this instead:

```
I want to build something but I'm still figuring out what. I have a vague idea about:
[one sentence about your general idea]

Help me explore it by asking me questions. Start with the most important question you'd ask someone who is early in the ideation phase of a project.
```

---

## Tips

- Answer Claude's questions as honestly and specifically as you can — vague answers lead to vague documents
- It's fine to say "I don't know yet" — Claude will help you figure out if that's something you need to decide now or later
- When you feel like you've covered the main ideas, ask Claude: "Can you help me summarize everything we've discussed into bullet points I can paste into my IDEATION.md?"

---

## What to Do After

Once your IDEATION.md has enough material, move to the [Product Overview prompt](./level-1-product-overview.md).
